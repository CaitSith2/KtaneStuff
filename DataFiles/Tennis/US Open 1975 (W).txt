Reigning champion [[Billie Jean King]] did not defend her title. After four consecutive semifinal finishes, [[Chris Evert]] defeated [[Evonne Goolagong]] 5–7, 6–4, 6–2 in the final to win the '''Women's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1975 US Open (tennis)|1975 US Open]].

[[Monica Seles]] was the three-time defending champion, but she was unable to compete due to being [[Monica Seles#1993 stabbing|stabbed]] the previous month.

[[Steffi Graf]] won her first French Open since 1988, defeating [[Mary Joe Fernández]] in the final. Following this tournament, Graf recaptured the [[List of WTA number 1 ranked tennis players|World No. 1]] ranking. It was also Fernández' third and final appearance in a Grand Slam singles final.

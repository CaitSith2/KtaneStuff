{{Main|1971 Wimbledon Championships}}
[[John Newcombe]] successfully defended his title, defeating [[Stan Smith]] in the final, 6–3, 5–7, 2–6, 6–4, 6–4 to win the Gentlemen's Singles tennis title at the 1971 Wimbledon Championships.<ref name="Barrett">{{cite book|last=Barrett|first=John|title=Wimbledon: The Official History|date=2014|publisher=Vision Sports Publishing|isbn=9-781909-534230|edition=4th}}</ref> It was Newcombe's third, and final, Wimbledon singles title.

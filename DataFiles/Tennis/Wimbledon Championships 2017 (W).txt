{{main|2017 Wimbledon Championships}}

[[Serena Williams]] was the two-time defending champion, but did not defend her title because of [[Serena Williams#2017: Australian Open victory and pregnancy|pregnancy]].<ref>{{cite web|url=http://edition.cnn.com/2017/04/19/sport/serena-williams-baby/|title=Serena Williams is pregnant, will return to tennis in 2018}}</ref>

[[Garbiñe Muguruza]] won her second Grand Slam singles title, defeating [[Venus Williams]] in the final, 7–5, 6–0.<ref>{{citeweb|url= https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2017/jul/15/garbine-muguruza-wimbledon-champion-venus-williams|title= Garbiñe Muguruza crowned Wimbledon champion after beating Venus Williams|date=15 July 2017|work=Guardian|accessdate=17 July 2017}}</ref>	
Muguruza became the second Spanish woman to win Wimbledon after [[Conchita Martínez]] in [[1994 Wimbledon Championships – Women's Singles|1994]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2017/07/15/sports/tennis/wimbledon-venus-williams-garbine-muguruza.html|title=Garbiñe Muguruza Wins Wimbledon, Defeating Venus Williams|publisher=The New York Times|author=Christopher Clarey|date=15 July 2017}}</ref> Muguruza also became the first player to defeat both [[Williams sisters]] in Grand Slam singles finals.<ref>{{citeweb|url= https://www.telegraph.co.uk/tennis/2017/07/15/venus-williams-vs-garbine-muguruza-wimbledon-2017-womens-final/|title= Garbine Muguruza hammers Venus Williams to win first Wimbledon title|date=15 July 2017|work=The Telegraph|accessdate=17 July 2017}}</ref>	

Williams was the oldest player to reach the final since [[Martina Navratilova]], also in 1994, and played her 100th singles match at Wimbledon when she defeated [[Jeļena Ostapenko]] in the quarterfinals.<ref>http://www.miamiherald.com/sports/tennis/article160489904.html</ref><ref>https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/tennis/40573123</ref> [[Johanna Konta]] became the first British woman to reach the semifinals since [[Virginia Wade]] in [[1978 Wimbledon Championships – Women's Singles|1978]],<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/sport/tennis/40574663|title=Wimbledon 2017: Johanna Konta beats Simona Halep to reach semi-final|publisher=BBC Sport|author=Piers Newbery|date=11 July 2017}}</ref> and [[Magdaléna Rybáriková]] became the first Slovak woman to reach the semifinals at Wimbledon.<ref>https://spectator.sme.sk/c/20601638/slovak-tennis-player-reaches-wimbledon-semi-finals.html</ref>

The tournament was the seventeenth consecutive women's singles Grand Slam event to feature a first-time semifinalist (Rybáriková). This streak ended months later at the [[2017 US Open – Women's Singles|2017 US Open]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://tennismash.com/2017/06/08/new-grand-slam-semifinalist/|title=A new Grand Slam semifinalist, again}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.sbnation.com/tennis/2017/9/6/16264632/2017-us-open-american-women-venus-williams-sloane-stephens-madison-keys-coco-vandeweghe|title=US Open 2017: 4 American women are in a Major semifinal for the first time since 1985}}</ref>

Despite losing in the second round, [[Karolína Plíšková]] attained the [[List of WTA number 1 ranked tennis players#Singles|WTA No. 1 singles ranking]] at the end of the tournament, after [[Angelique Kerber]] and [[Simona Halep]], the other contenders for the top ranking, lost in the fourth round and quarterfinals, respectively.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.wtatennis.com/news/karolina-pliskova-wta-world-no1-ranking-angelique-kerber-wimbledon|title=Here’s how Karolina Pliskova can become World No.1|publisher=Women's Tennis Association|date=3 July 2017}}</ref>

This was [[Victoria Azarenka]]'s first appearance in a Grand Slam event since the [[2016 French Open – Women's Singles|2016 French Open]] after giving birth to her first child in December 2016.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/sport/2017/jul/01/victoria-azarenka-wimbledon-tennis|title=Victoria Azarenka ‘fitter than ever’ for Wimbledon after maternity break|publisher=The Observer|date=1 July 2017|author=Kevin Mitchell}}</ref> She reached the fourth round before losing to Halep.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-tennis-wimbledon-halep/tennis-halep-dashes-new-mum-azarenkas-comeback-dreams-idUSKBN19V1XN|title=Tennis: Halep dashes new mum Azarenka's comeback dreams|publisher=Reuteurs|date=10 July 2017}}</ref>

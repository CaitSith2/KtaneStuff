[[Ilie Nastase]] was the defending champion but lost in the quarterfinals to [[Harold Solomon]].

[[Björn Borg]] defeated [[Manuel Orantes]] 2–6, 6–7<sup>(4–7)</sup>, 6–0, 6–1, 6–1 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1974 French Open]]. The format was slightly unusual for a Men's Grand Slam event: the first two rounds were played best-of-three sets, while the last five rounds were played as best-of-five sets.

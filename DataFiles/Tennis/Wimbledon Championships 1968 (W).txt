{{main|1968 Wimbledon Championships}}

[[Billie Jean King]] was the two-time defending champion and successfully defended her title, defeating [[Judy Tegart-Dalton|Judy Tegart]] in the final, 9–7, 7–5.<ref name="Barrett">{{cite book|last=Barrett|first=John|title=Wimbledon: The Official History|date=2014|publisher=Vision Sports Publishing|isbn=9-781909-534230|edition=4th}}</ref> This was King's second Grand Slam title of the year and her fifth overall.

[[Manuel Orantes]] defeated defending champion [[Jimmy Connors]] 6–4, 6–3, 6–3 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1975 US Open (tennis)|1975 US Open]].

==Format==
Unlike the previous year, the first three rounds were best of three sets.

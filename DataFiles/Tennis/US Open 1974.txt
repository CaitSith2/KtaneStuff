[[John Newcombe]] was the defending champion but lost in the semifinals to [[Ken Rosewall]].

[[Jimmy Connors]] defeated [[Ken Rosewall]] 6–1, 6–0, 6–1 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1974 U.S. Open (tennis)|1974 US Open]].<ref>{{cite book|last=Collins|first=Bud|title=The Bud Collins History of Tennis|year=2010|publisher=New Chapter Press|location=[New York]|isbn=978-0942257700|page=462|edition=2nd}}</ref> It set the record for the shortest ever Grand Slam men's singles final, both in number of games and duration, taking only 1 hour and 18 minutes to complete.

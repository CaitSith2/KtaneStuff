[[Jan Kodes]] was the two-time defending champion, but lost in the quarterfinals to [[Patrick Proisy]].

[[Andrés Gimeno]] defeated [[Patrick Proisy]] 4–6, 6–3, 6–1, 6–1 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1972 French Open]].

[[John McEnroe]] was the three time defending champion but lost in the semifinals to [[Ivan Lendl]].

[[Jimmy Connors]] defeated [[Ivan Lendl]] 6–3, 6–2, 4–6, 6–4 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1982 US Open (tennis)|1982 US Open]].

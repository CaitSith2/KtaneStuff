[[Margaret Court]] was the defending champion, and successfully defended her title, defeating [[Rosie Casals]] in the final, 6–2, 2–6, 6–1. This was Court's fourth US Open title, her fifth consecutive Grand Slam title and her twentieth Grand Slam overall. By holding the four major titles at the same time, Court became the first woman in the [[Open Era]] to complete the [[Grand Slam (tennis)|Calendar Grand Slam]].

[[Andy Roddick]] was the defending champion, but lost in the quarterfinals against [[Joachim Johansson]].

[[Roger Federer]] won the title, defeating [[2001 US Open (tennis)|2001]] champion [[Lleyton Hewitt]], 6–0, 7–6<sup>(7–3)</sup>, 6–0, in the final. This was Federer's first US Open title, and made him the tenth man to win three Grand Slams in a calendar year.

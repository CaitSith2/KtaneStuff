{{Main|1972 Wimbledon Championships}}
[[John Newcombe]] was prevented from defending his title due to the [[International Tennis Federation|International Lawn Tennis Federation]] ban on [[World Championship Tennis]] contract players competing in their tournaments.<ref>{{cite book|title=The Bud Collins History of Tennis|year=2010|publisher=New Chapter Press|location=[New York]|isbn=978-0942257700|page=160|author=[[Bud Collins]]|edition=2nd}}</ref>

[[Stan Smith]] defeated [[Ilie Năstase]] in the final, 4–6, 6–3, 6–3, 4–6, 7–5 to win the Gentlemen's Singles tennis title at the 1972 Wimbledon Championships.<ref name="Barrett">{{cite book|last=Barrett|first=John|title=Wimbledon: The Official History|date=2014|publisher=Vision Sports Publishing|isbn=9-781909-534230|edition=4th}}</ref> It was Smith's first, and only, Wimbledon singles title, and his second, and final, grand slam singles title.

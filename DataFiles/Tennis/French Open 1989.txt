[[Mats Wilander]] was the defending champion, but he was upset in the quarterfinals by [[Andrei Chesnokov]].

[[Michael Chang]] won the title, and as such broke the record for the youngest-ever Grand Slam – Men's Singles champion at the age of {{Age in years, months and days|1972|2|22|1989|6|11}}.  Among his victories was a [[Michael_Chang#1989_French_Open_match_vs_Ivan_Lendl|defeat of World No. 1 and three-time French Open champion Ivan Lendl]], which is remembered as one of the most significant matches in French Open history.

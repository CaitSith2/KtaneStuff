[[Yevgeny Kafelnikov]] was the defending champion, but he was defeated by unseeded Brazilian [[Gustavo Kuerten]] (ranked no. 66) in the quarterfinals.

Kuerten went on to win the tournament and as well as his first ATP title, defeating [[Sergi Bruguera]] in the final. At the time, Kuerten was the second-lowest ranked player to win a Grand Slam and the first Brazilian. As a result, Kuerten moved up to number 15 in the rankings following his win.

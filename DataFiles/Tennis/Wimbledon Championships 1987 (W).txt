{{Main|1987 Wimbledon Championships}}
[[Martina Navratilova]] successfully defended her title, defeating [[Steffi Graf]] in the final, 7–5, 6–3 to win the Ladies' Singles tennis title at the 1987 Wimbledon Championships, her 6th consecutive title.<ref name="Barrett">{{cite book|last=Barrett|first=John|title=Wimbledon: The Official History|date=2014|publisher=Vision Sports Publishing|isbn=9-781909-534230|edition=4th}}</ref>

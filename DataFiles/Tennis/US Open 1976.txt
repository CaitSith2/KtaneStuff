[[Manuel Orantes]] was the defending champion but lost in the quarterfinals to [[Björn Borg]].

[[Jimmy Connors]] defeated [[Björn Borg]] 6–4, 3–6, 7–6<sup>(11–9)</sup>, 6–4 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1976 US Open (tennis)|1976 US Open]] despite scoring fewer points in total (121) than his opponent (123).<ref>{{cite magazine |title=The big rackets |magazine=[[Sports Illustrated]] |date=February 17, 1977 |page=65 |url=https://www.si.com/vault/issue/41219/65}}</ref>

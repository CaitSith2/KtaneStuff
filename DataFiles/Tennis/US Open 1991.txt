[[Pete Sampras]] was the defending champion in the men's singles tournament at the  [[1991 US Open (tennis)|1991 US Open]], but lost in the quarterfinals to [[Jim Courier]]. [[Stefan Edberg]] defeated Courier 6–2, 6–4, 6–0 in the final to win the title

==Events==
* Top seed and 1989 champion [[Boris Becker]] was eliminated in the third round by [[Paul Haarhuis]].
* Perennial favorite [[Andre Agassi]] was eliminated in the first round by [[Aaron Krickstein]].
* Five-time champion [[Jimmy Connors]], who turned 39 during the tournament, entered the tournament as a wildcard and made it to the semifinals before losing to eventual runner-up [[Jim Courier]].

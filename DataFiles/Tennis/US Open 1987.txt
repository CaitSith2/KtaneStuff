[[Ivan Lendl]] was the two time defending champion and retained his title again, defeating [[Mats Wilander]] 6–7, 6–0, 7–6, 6–4 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1987 US Open (tennis)|1987 US Open]].  It was also the 6th consecutive US Open final of Lendl's record 8 consecutive US Open finals.

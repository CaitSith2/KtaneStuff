[[Steffi Graf]] was the defending champion, but she retired from professional tennis on 13 August 1999.

[[Mary Pierce]] defeated [[Conchita Martínez]] 6–2, 7–5 in the final to win her second and last Grand Slam title.

Pierce became the first French female player to win her home Grand Slam since [[Françoise Durr]] won it in 1967.

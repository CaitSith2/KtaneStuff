[[Andres Gimeno]] was the defending champion, but lost in the second round to future champion [[Guillermo Vilas]].

[[Ilie Năstase]] defeated [[Nikola Pilić]] 6–3, 6–3, 6–0 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1973 French Open]]. The tournament was also the first Grand Slam that [[Björn Borg]] competed in.

Two-time reigning champion [[Margaret Court]] did not defend her title. [[Billie Jean King]] defeated [[Rosemary Casals]] 6–4, 7–6 (5-2) in the final to win the '''Women's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1971 US Open (tennis)|1971 US Open]].

This tournament was notable for being the first Grand Slam appearance in which [[Chris Evert]] competed in the main draw, she made it to the semi-final. She competed in 19 consecutive US Open tournaments until her retirement in [[1989 US Open – Women's Singles|1989]].

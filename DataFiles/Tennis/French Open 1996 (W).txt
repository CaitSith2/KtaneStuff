[[Steffi Graf]] was the defending champion and successfully defended her title, defeating [[Arantxa Sánchez Vicario]] 6–3, 6–7<sup>(4–7)</sup>, 10–8 in the final on the second consecutive year. By winning her 19th Grand Slam title, Graf surpassed [[Chris Evert]] and [[Martina Navratilova]]'s [[List of Grand Slam related tennis records#Most singles titles (Open Era)|all time record overall]] [[List of Grand Slam women's singles champions#Open Era (2 or more)|Grand Slam singles titles at the Open Era]].

{{main article|2010 US Open (tennis)}}

[[Kim Clijsters]] was the defending champion, and successfully defended her 2009 title, after she won 6–2, 6–1, against [[Vera Zvonareva]] in the final.

[[List of WTA number 1 ranked tennis players|World No. 1]] and three-time champion [[Serena Williams]] withdrew from the tournament due to foot surgery sustained following from an exhibition match in [[Munich]]. This promoted defending finalist [[Caroline Wozniacki]] to top seed and she was in contention to gain the WTA no. 1 ranking by winning the title, but lost to Zvonareva in the semifinals.

This was the final Grand Slam appearance for 2004 US Open finalist and former World No. 3 [[Elena Dementieva]], she was defeated in the fourth round by [[Samantha Stosur]].

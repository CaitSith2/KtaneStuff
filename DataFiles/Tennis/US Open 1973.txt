[[Ilie Nastase]] was the defending champion but lost in the second round to [[Andrew Pattison]].

[[John Newcombe]] defeated [[Jan Kodeš]] 6–4, 1–6, 4–6, 6–2, 6–3 in the final to win the men's singles title at the [[1973 US Open (tennis)|1973 US Open]].

{{main|2003 French Open}}

[[Albert Costa]] was the defending champion, but lost in the semifinals to [[Juan Carlos Ferrero]], who ultimately won in the final 6–1, 6–3, 6–2, against [[Martin Verkerk]]. As of {{CURRENTYEAR}}, this is the last time [[Roger Federer]] lost in the first round of a Grand Slam.

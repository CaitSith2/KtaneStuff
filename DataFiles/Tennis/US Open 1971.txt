[[Ken Rosewall]] was the defending champion but did not compete at the 1971 US Open – Men's Singles event.

[[Stan Smith]] defeated [[Jan Kodeš]] 3–6, 6–3, 6–2, 7–6<sup>(5–3)</sup> in the final to win the men's singles title at the [[1971 US Open (tennis)|1971 US Open]].

[[Martina Hingis]] was the defending champion, but lost in the final to [[Lindsay Davenport]], 6–3, 7–5, in a rematch of last year's semifinal.

The tournament was notable for the final US Open appearance of five-time champion [[Steffi Graf]] in the main draw, who lost to [[Patty Schnyder]] in the fourth round. It was also the first US Open appearance of six-time champion [[Serena Williams]], where she won her first title in the following year.

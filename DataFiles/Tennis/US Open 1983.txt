[[Jimmy Connors]] was the defending champion and retained his title, defeating [[Ivan Lendl]] 6–3, 6–7, 7–5, 6–0 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1983 US Open (tennis)|1983 US Open]]. That was Connors's 100th ATP single title. By 2018, he is the only male player to have achieved a three-digit number of ATP singles titles.

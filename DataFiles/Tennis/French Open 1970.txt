[[Rod Laver]] was the defending champion, but did not compete this year.

[[Jan Kodeš]] defeated [[Željko Franulović]] 6–2, 6–4, 6–0 in the final to win the '''Men's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1970 French Open]].

[[Monica Seles]] was the two-time defending champion, she was unable to participate due to a [[Monica Seles#1993 stabbing|stabbing]] incident in April of this year.

[[Steffi Graf]] defeated [[Helena Suková]] 6–3, 6–3 in the final to win the '''Women's Singles''' tennis title at the [[1993 U.S. Open (tennis)|1993 U.S. Open]]. It was Graf's third U.S. Open title and Suková's fourth and final appearance in a Grand Slam final.
